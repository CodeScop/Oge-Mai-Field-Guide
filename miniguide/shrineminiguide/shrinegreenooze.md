LAST UPDATED 23 MAR 2020

Remember, to see the following, your avatar needs to be wearing the Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet, which can be purchased for 5 Shrine Tickets in the Shrine Bracelets area.

# Quests - Green Ooze

### Green Ooze Invasion

- **Unlocked by:** Wardrobe: Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet
- **Type:** Battle
- **Requirements:** Defeat 1 each:
  - Mini Ooze
  - Tryi Ooze
  - Wowtri Ooze
  - Balli Ooze
  - Froji Ooze
  - Whaly Ooze
  - Yawni Ooze
  - Wavi Ooze
  - Giant Ooze
  - Giant Ooze House
- **Rewards:** 50 Shrine Stones
- **Recommendations:** Look at all these entries for your bestiary! Look at all the Shrine Stones! You've got this!

### Seeing a Sequence

- **Unlocked by:** Wardrobe: Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet
- **Type:** Battle
- **Requirements:** Defeat the following:
  - 1 Giant Ooze
  - 2 Giant Ooze Houses
  - 3 Wavi Ooze
  - 5 Wowtri Ooze
  - 8 Froji Ooze
- **Rewards:** 25 Shrine Stones
- **Recommendations:** Make sure you've picked up Green Ooze Invasion to get max effectiveness.

### Mini OoOoOoOozes

- **Unlocked by:** Wardrobe: Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet
- **Type:** Battle
- **Requirements:** Defeat 100 Mini Oozes
- **Rewards:** Wardrobe: Mini Ooze Pet https://media.4thewords.com/s3/media/wardrobe/featured/male/wm&#95;featured&#95;ooze&#95;pet.png
- **Recommendations:** It's 100 battles, but only 5K battle-words. And that's a cute pet.

### Cleaning The House

- **Unlocked by:** Wardrobe: Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet
- **Type:** Streak, Battle
- **Requirements:** Maintain a 5-day streak; defeat 5 Giant Ooze Houses
- **Rewards:** 25 Shrine Stones
- **Recommendations:** I'm completionist; I'm doing this. And it's really not that hard.

### There She Blows

- **Unlocked by:** Wardrobe: Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet
- **Type:** Battle
- **Requirements:** Defeat 10 Whaly Ooze
- **Rewards:** 25 Shrine Stones
- **Recommendations:** Don't forget about the Green Ooze Invasion quest while you're here!

### Blobby was Here

- **Unlocked by:** Wardrobe: Green Ooze Shrine Bracelet
- **Type:** Battle
- **Requirements:** Defeat 3 Giant Oozes
- **Rewards:** 10 Shrine Stones
- **Recommendations:** This is a pretty small reward for the battles involved, but on the upside, you *also* get the rewards for battling the Giant Oozes on the way.

# Resources - Green Ooze

### Dust

- **Best sources:** 
  - Anywhere Battles: Xin (100 words for 1-2), Awa (200 words for 1-2)
- **Other sources:** 
  - Shrine GO Battles: Balli Ooze (250 words for 0-2)
- **Buy:** 
  - FOREST:  MT Item Shop/Edrie's Market: 8 Coins (not worth it)
  - DESERT: 12 Coins in Item Bazaar/Zilya's Great Bazaar (don't bother)
- **Sell price:** 2 Coins (don't sell unless you have >1000)
- **Used for:** outside game

### Leaves

- **Sources:** FOREST, DESERT, WATER
  - Anywhere Battles: Xin (100 words for 1-2), Awa (200 words for 1-2), Vi (5 minutes for 1-4)
  - Shrine GO Battles: Wowtri Ooze (250 words for 0-2), Balli Ooze (250 words for 0-1)
- **Buy:** FOREST
- **Sell price:** 2 Coins (DO NOT SELL DO NOT SELL THERE IS NO SUCH THINGS AS TOO MANY LEAVES)
- **Used for:** outside game

### Raw Steel

- **Sources:** FOREST EXPEDITION, DESERT, WATER,
  - Shrine GO Battles: Giant Ooze House (500 words for 0-3)
- **Buy:** DESERT
- **Sell price:** 8 Coins (DO NOT SELL. You will need this to craft SO MANY STEEL BARS.)
- **Used for:** outside game

### Raw Stone

- **Best sources:** DESERT,
  - Anywhere Battles: Xin (100 words for 0-1) 
- **Other sources:** FOREST, WATER,
  - Shrine GO Battles: Tryi Ooze (150 words for 0-1), Giant Ooze (1000 words for 1-4)
- **Buy:** DESERT: 35 Coins at 3PC Blacksmith/Blacksmith Barliar
- **Sell price:** 8 Coins (don't sell unless you have >100)
- **Used for:** outside game

### Red Dye

- **Sources:** DESERT, WATER,
  - Shrine GO Battles: Wavi Ooze (330 words for 0-1)
- **Buy:** DESERT
- **Sell price:** 15 Coins (don't sell unless you have >100; will usually be better to use to craft Fine Fabric and sell for more)
- **Used for:** outside game

### Rope

- **Best sources:** 
  - Anywhere Battles: Awa (200 words for 2)
- **Other sources:** Buy,
  - Shrine GO Battles: Whaly Ooze (100 words for 0-1), Giant Ooze House (500 words for 1-2), Giant Ooze (1000 words for 1-2)
- **Buy:** FOREST, WATER 8 Coins in MT Item Shop/Edrie's Market OR Riza Item Shop/Luko's Inn, DESERT 12 Coins in 3PC Item Bazaar/Zilya's Great Bazaar
- **Sell price:** 2 Coins (DO NOT SELL, there is NEVER enough Rope)
- **Used for:** outside game

### Shrine Stone

- **Sources:** 
  - Shrine GO Battles: Mini Ooze (50 words for 1), Froji Ooze (165 words for 2-3), Whaly Ooze (100 words for 1), Yawni Ooze (125 words for 1-2), Tryi Ooze (150 words for 1-2), Wowtri Ooze (250 words for 2-3), Balli Ooze (250 words for 2-3), Wavi Ooze (330 words for 3-4), Giant Ooze House (500 words for 5), Giant Ooze (1000 words for 10)
